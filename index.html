What is this

A tool to identify and analyse various audio formats. View bitrate and encoder info, spectrogram and bitrate graphs etc

Currently in Alpha release (that means it might not work)

Supports: MP3, AAC, Vorbis, FLAC, Opus, Musepack, Wav and more
